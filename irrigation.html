Project Title

Construction of Irrigation Canals & Water Supply Scheme within Doha Municipality (Zone 15, 18, 21), including associated civil, mechanical, and electrical works.

Scope of Work

  • Excavation, earthworks, and construction of concrete/HDPE-lined irrigation canals (total length: 25.2km)
  • Supply and installation of primary and secondary water distribution pipelines (various diameters) and appurtenances
  • Construction of regulating structures (gates, culverts, turnouts) and flow-measurement devices
  • Pumping stations, control panels, and SCADA-ready instrumentation
  • Landscaping and reinstatement of affected areas
  • Testing, commissioning, training of Employer's personnel, and handover
